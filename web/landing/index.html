The problem

Bargaining is the buying process.

For millions of small businesses, a sale isn't a fixed-price checkout — it's a negotiation, over WhatsApp and across the counter, in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil and everything in between.

The owner is the only one who knows how far a price can move — and the owner can't be in every chat, every evening, all season. So staff either hold firm and lose the deal, or give away margin with no record of how the price was reached.

63M+
micro & small businesses in India sell by negotiation
Fixed-price chatbots don't fit how they trade.
The difference

Meet the AI sales agent.

Not a fixed-price bot — a negotiator. It speaks the customer's language, concedes on price only within limits you set, and knows exactly when to call you in.

🗣️

Speaks their language

Hindi, Telugu, Tamil or English — script and tone mirrored, strictly one language at a time. It sounds like a real salesperson, not a translator.

⚖️

Negotiates within your limits

You set the floor price and max discount. The agent concedes one step at a time, each with a reason — and structurally cannot go below what you allow.

🙋

Escalates, never improvises

Below your floor, an order too small, a total too big — the chat pauses and pings you for one-tap approval. You stay in control of the risky calls.

🧾

Closes and bills

On a yes, it confirms the exact agreed price, prints a letterheaded receipt, and hands the order off to WhatsApp for delivery or pickup.
